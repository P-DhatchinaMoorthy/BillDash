POST /products/
- Create single product
- Create multiple products (bulk)
- Required fields: id, product_name, sku, purchase_price

GET /products/
- List all products
- Optional filters: category_id, subcategory_id
- Examples:
  GET /products/
  GET /products/?category_id=1
  GET /products/?subcategory_id=2
  GET /products/?category_id=1&subcategory_id=2

GET /products/<int:product_id>
- Get single product by ID
- Example: GET /products/1010

PUT /products/<int:product_id>
- Update product by ID

POST /products/bulk
- Bulk upload via CSV or XLSX files
- Supports batch product creation
