GET Requests:
- List all purchases: http://localhost:5000/purchase-billing/
- Filter all by payment status: http://localhost:5000/purchase-billing/?payment_status=partially paid
- Purchase details: http://localhost:5000/purchase-billing/{purchase_id}
- Supplier purchases: http://localhost:5000/purchase-billing/supplier/{supplier_id}
- Filter supplier by payment status: http://localhost:5000/purchase-billing/supplier/{supplier_id}?payment_status=paid
- Purchase adjustments: http://localhost:5000/purchase-billing/adjustment
- Adjustment details: http://localhost:5000/purchase-billing/adjustment/{adjustment_id}

POST Requests:
- Add Stock (Purchase): http://localhost:5000/purchases/add-stock
- Pay Supplier: http://localhost:5000/purchase-billing/pay-supplier
- Purchase Return: http://localhost:5000/purchase-billing/return
- Purchase Adjustment: http://localhost:5000/purchase-billing/adjustment

PUT Requests:
- Update Purchase Payment: http://localhost:5000/purchases/update-payment/{purchase_id}
- Update Payment Status: http://localhost:5000/purchase-billing/{bill_id}/status

Stock Reports URLs:
- Stock movement report: http://localhost:5000/reports/stock-movement
- Filter by location: http://localhost:5000/reports/stock-movement?location=Branch1
- Reorder report: http://localhost:5000/reports/reorder
- Low stock alerts: http://localhost:5000/products/low-stock
